A Little Bit About Me

My name is Sydnee Martin, and I'm in my late 20s. I received my Bachelor's of Arts degree from The George Washington University in Washington, D.C. in American Studies. This is my last semester--three days!--at the University of Florida, where I will receive a Master of Arts degree in Communications, specializing in Global Strategic Communications.

I live in Virginia with my three-year-old dog, Harlow, and recently bought my first home. I currently work for the USDA Forest Service. I have quite a few hobbies, which you can see listed to the left.
